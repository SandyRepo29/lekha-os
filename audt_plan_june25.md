**Date:** June 2026

# Executive Summary

AUDT has successfully completed Phase 1 of its journey.

Phase 1 focused on building the product.

Today AUDT includes:

* 32 integrated platform modules
* Vendor Governance
* Trust Operations
* Risk & Compliance
* Trust Intelligence
* AI Governance
* Executive Reporting
* Governance Agents
* Continuous Compliance

The focus now shifts from **building more modules** to **building an enterprise-ready business and category-leading platform**.

The roadmap is organized into four parallel tracks.
